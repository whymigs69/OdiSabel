About Us

Arellano University

The Arellano University seal was inspired by the heroic photograph of American marines raising the Stars and Stripes on the summit of mount Surabachi. The seal was prepared by the great artist, Vicente Manansala. The whole design is expressive of the faith of the Arellano University in the youth of the land as builders of the Filipino nation.

Arellano University prides itself as an institution dedicated to addressing the needs of the global market. Beyond marrying the skills needed in today’s competitive market to academic excellence, the university also prioritizes industry needs, experience, and growth for its students.

With this as its mission, Arellano University has linked itself with local industry giants through various partnerships over the years. Across all disciplines, the university maintains its mission in connecting students with the relevant opportunities in their specific fields.

It doesn’t stop there.

Under Arellano University’s International Programs, the university is a partner to several other foreign universities in places such as the United States, Australia, Japan, and Thailand. As the university believes in providing equitable access to education that is globally competitive, Arellano University consistently updates their offerings based on the needs of their students and the job market.
