ETEEAP

The Expanded Tertiary Education Equivalency and Accreditation Program (ETEEAP) of the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) as a comprehensive educational assessment scheme recognizes knowledge and prior learning obtained from non-formal and informal experiences. Through this program, a college diploma is granted as a degree based on competency evaluation and a comprehensive assessment system which utilizes written tests, interviews, skills demonstrations, portfolio and other creative and appropriate assessment methodologies.
